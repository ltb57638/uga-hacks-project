How It Works

FortuneTLR is a machine learning model that uses positive/negative words combined with past stock price trends and gives a recommendation on if the stock should be bought or sold.

How to Use FortuneTLR

Find a current article related to the stock in question

Paste the first 100 words of the article into the homepage of FortuneTLR

Recieve your up to date stock advice!
